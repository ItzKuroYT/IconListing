About GroundZero

## 🧟 Welcome to GroundZero

GroundZero is a **modded Zombie Survival Roleplay** experience where **mods and custom plugin systems work together** to create one connected world.

Choose your own path. Become a **Guard**, build a **drug empire**, defend valuable **convoys**, run your own **business**, explore dangerous **POIs**, uncover hidden secrets, or simply fight to survive in a world where every decision matters.

Unlike traditional servers, GroundZero is built through **live development**. The community helps shape the future of the server as we continue expanding the world with new systems, cities and content.

## 🌍 Features

* 🧟 Modded Zombie Survival
* 🚓 Player-Driven Police System
* 💊 Drug Production & Black Market
* 🚚 Convoy Missions
* ⛏️ Prison System
* 🏗️ Community City Unlocks
* 🏚️ Base Building & Raiding
* ⚔️ PvP & Red Zones
* 🔫 Modded Weapons
* 🌾 Farming, Fishing & Trading
* 🎭 Optional Roleplay

## 🚀 How to Join

Joining GroundZero is easy!
